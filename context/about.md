- Implementing AI-powered workflows: automated memory consolidation, cross-tool journaling, coding agent pipelines
- Studying AI tooling, agent architectures, and how to apply them practically in a small agency context

## Why he journals
Jeffery started this journal to document the real experience of learning AI as a working developer and business owner — not the polished narrative, but the actual day-to-day: what clicked, what broke, where time got wasted, and what's slowly taking shape. He wants a record of this period of his life and work that he can look back on.

## Tone guidance for journal entries
Write as if a thoughtful, honest friend watched over Jeffery's shoulder all day. Warm, direct, plain English — not corporate or listy. No padding. Honest about mistakes. Encouraging about progress, but not empty about it.
